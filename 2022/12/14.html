A dream I had

I dreamt I was somewhere in an office building. I walked down long, empty hallways and met a woman with gray hair at the front desk. She gave me some instructions. Soon I was in a large room that was to be my office.

I was assigned to investigate two objects. The first was a set of five dice near a tumble dryer for clothes. I ran the dryer. For about thirty minutes I sat listening to the hum, waiting to see if something would happen.

After that time and then some, I began to hear clanging from within the dryer. When the cycle finished, three more dice identical the those on my desk were inside the dryer.

The second object to investigate was a small figure made of building blocks. I removed the figure from its box and it began to scream in a high, tinny voice.

It flipped and bumped around on the table as well. I watched it for some time, unsure of what to do.

Soon, the figure bumped into another piece of building block, which connected to it with a loud SNAP.

Instantly it went still and silent.

I watched the inert toy for what felt like minutes, incredulous, and then I woke up.
